# AIS Trading Inc Logo

This folder contains the company logo in various formats:

## Files:

- **logo.svg** - Vector format logo (used as favicon)
- **logo-full.svg** - Full logo with gradient and tree icon

## Logo Specs:

- Primary Color: #1A7A3C (Forest Green)
- Secondary Color: #2EAA57 (Light Green)
- Icon: Stylized tree/timber representing lumber industry
- Shape: Rounded square with gradient background

## Usage:

The logo.svg is automatically used as the browser favicon (tab icon).

To export as JPG:

1. Open logo.svg in any image editor (Photoshop, GIMP, etc.)
2. Export/Save As JPG at desired resolution (recommended: 512x512px or 1024x1024px)
3. Save as: logo.jpg or ais-trading-logo.jpg

Alternatively, you can use an online converter like cloudconvert.com or convertio.co to convert the SVG to JPG.
